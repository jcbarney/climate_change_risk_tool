The Coastal Real Estate Risk Calculator is based on the current trajectory of rising sea levels and assumes that the CO2 emmissions continue to increase at the current rate. The calculator only takes the elevation at a specific location into account and doesn't look at surrounding higher elevations so there is a risk of false positives (there are many dry places in the world that are at or below sea level without direct access to the ocean). This calculator is not for professional use.
